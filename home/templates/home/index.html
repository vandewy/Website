About:

I have been an Air Traffic controller for the past 10 years; rated as a RAPCON controller as well as a Certified Tower Controller for the United States Air Force in Texas and Alaska. I have also spent 4 years as an Air Traffic Control instructor, teaching the fundamentals of FAA and USAF rules and regulations via lecture as well as in a simulated environment. As an instructor I also taught the Standard Terminal Automations Replacement System (STARS) course. This course include basic system admin responsibilies in a Unix (Solaris 10) environment and the use of security software such as NISPlus and the Basic Security Module for auditing.


I decided to pursue a degree in Computer Science; I have since found myself spending most of my free time working on software projects, particularly in the area of air traffic control simulations using the Unity game engine
